WHO WE
ARE

The Neckfire team is a long-prepared enterprise that aims to bring together any experts or apprentices in a friendly environment where everyone will develop not only their knowledge, but their skills in passing on knowledge to allow everyone to access the world of computing that is still too little known, or too superficially. We wish to reconcile open source and owners, large companies and small ones, to open the knowledge to the world in order to enter an era of cooperation and not of competition, so that this set can finally innovate quickly and at lower cost.

how we wants to do that ?


We wants to create a new shape of enterprise, organized to have one head group that manage all the others, and all these groups make specific domains. These group are a workspace for two kind of people :
- employee
- students
We wants also to make our entreprise a school place, to train new employee, or students, we wants to share our knowledges, our research, and break the walls between other businesses.
For the moment, we have only 2 objectives, create the Neckfire Team, and start building ours projects, and continue with ARPON OS
